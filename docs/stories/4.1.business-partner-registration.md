# Story 4: Business Partner Registration & Onboarding

## Status

In Progress

## Story

**As a** Service Provider/Business Owner,
**I want** to register my business on the Cleanly platform and create a professional profile,
**so that** I can offer my services to customers, manage bookings, and grow my business through the marketplace.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Business Registration Flow**: Multi-step registration process for service providers
2. **Business Profile Creation**: Comprehensive business profile with services, pricing, and availability
3. **Service Management**: Add, edit, and manage service offerings with categories and pricing
4. **Business Authentication**: Separate login system for business accounts
5. **Professional Dashboard**: Business-focused dashboard for managing operations
6. **Verification System**: Business verification and approval process
7. **CleanEase UI**: Consistent design system with customer-facing app
8. **Mobile Responsive**: Works seamlessly on all device sizes

## Design Requirements

### **Visual Style (CleanEase Business)**

- **Professional Interface**: Clean, business-focused design with productivity in mind
- **Brand Consistency**: Same color palette and design language as customer app
- **Business Branding**: Allow businesses to showcase their own branding within limits
- **Dashboard Layout**: Clear navigation, quick stats, and action-oriented interface
- **Form Design**: Multi-step forms with clear progress indicators
- **Status Indicators**: Clear visual feedback for approval status, booking states, etc.

### **User Experience Flow**

1. **Discovery**: Business learns about Cleanly partnership
2. **Registration**: Multi-step signup with business details
3. **Profile Setup**: Add services, pricing, availability, and branding
4. **Verification**: Submit documents for platform approval
5. **Dashboard Access**: Access business dashboard once approved
6. **Service Management**: Ongoing management of services and bookings

## Technical Implementation

### **Database Schema**

- **Business Profile**: Extended profile data for business accounts
- **Service Catalog**: Business services with pricing and availability
- **Business Settings**: Operational preferences and configurations
- **Verification Records**: Documents and approval status tracking

### **Authentication System**

- **Business User Context**: Separate from customer context with different permissions
- **Role-Based Access**: Business owner, employee, admin roles
- **Multi-tenant Support**: Multiple businesses, multiple users per business

### **API Endpoints**

- `business.register()` - Business registration with profile creation
- `business.getProfile()` - Get business profile and settings
- `business.updateProfile()` - Update business information
- `services.create()` - Create new service offering
- `services.getByBusiness()` - Get all services for a business
- `bookings.getForBusiness()` - Get bookings for business management
